# Refund Policy

## Overview
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free refund process.

## Refund Eligibility
You are eligible for a refund if:
- You have not used or damaged the product
- You initiate the refund within 30 days of purchase
- You have the original receipt or order confirmation
- The product is in its original packaging

## Items Not Eligible for Refund
The following items cannot be refunded:
- Customized or personalized items
- Items marked as "Final Sale"
- Products purchased during clearance events
- Digital downloads or software licenses
- Items damaged due to customer negligence

## Refund Process
1. Contact our customer service team at refunds@company.com
2. Provide your order number and reason for return
3. We will provide a prepaid return shipping label
4. Ship the item back to us (at no cost to you)
5. Once received and inspected, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days

## Refund Amount
- Full refunds are issued for unopened items in original condition
- Restocking fee of 10% applies to opened/used items
- Shipping costs are non-refundable

## Refund Method
Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for purchase. Depending on your bank, it may take 5-10 additional business days for the refund to appear.

## Special Cases
- If an item is defective, we offer full refunds regardless of condition
- Holiday purchases made within 15 days before any holiday can be refunded until 30 days after the holiday
- Bulk orders may have different refund terms as outlined in the order agreement

## Questions
Contact us at support@company.com for any refund-related questions.
