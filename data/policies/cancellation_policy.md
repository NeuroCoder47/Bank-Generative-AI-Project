# Cancellation Policy

## Order Cancellation Window
You can cancel your order at any time before it is dispatched:
- Orders are dispatched within 1 business day of placement
- You must request cancellation before the dispatch time (typically 2 PM EST)
- Cancellation requests received after dispatch cannot be honored

## How to Cancel
1. Log into your account on our website
2. Go to "Order History"
3. Select the order you wish to cancel
4. Click "Request Cancellation"
5. Select a reason (optional)
6. Confirm cancellation request

Alternatively, email cancel@company.com with your order number.

## Cancellation Processing
- Cancellation requests are processed within 2 hours
- If processed before dispatch, no charges will apply
- You will receive a confirmation email within 1 hour

## If Order Already Shipped
- If your order has already been dispatched, you cannot cancel it
- You will instead need to follow our Return/Refund Policy
- Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase

## Non-Cancellable Orders
The following orders cannot be cancelled:
- Custom or personalized items that have begun production
- Digital products or downloads that have been accessed
- Items on final sale or clearance
- Orders that have already been shipped

## Cancellation and Refunds
- Full refund is issued to the original payment method
- Processing typically takes 1-2 business days
- Depending on your bank, the refund may take 5-10 business days to appear

## Subscription Cancellations
- Recurring subscriptions can be cancelled anytime
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle
- No refunds are issued for the current billing period
- To cancel, go to "Subscription Management" in your account

## Recurring Orders
- You can cancel recurring orders anytime in the order history
- Changes take effect on the next scheduled order date
- Previous orders cannot be cancelled (see refund policy instead)

## International Orders
- International orders follow the same cancellation window as domestic
- However, if already in transit, cancellation may not be possible
- Contact our support team for assistance with international orders

## Questions
For cancellation questions or issues, contact support@company.com.
