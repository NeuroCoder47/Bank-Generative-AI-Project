# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Methods
We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

### Standard Shipping
- Delivery time: 5-7 business days
- Cost: FREE for orders over $50 (otherwise $5.99)
- Tracking: Available after order dispatch

### Express Shipping
- Delivery time: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $15.99
- Tracking: Real-time tracking included

### Overnight Shipping
- Delivery time: Next business day (if ordered before 2 PM)
- Cost: $29.99
- Tracking: Real-time tracking included

### International Shipping
- Delivery time: 10-21 business days depending on destination
- Cost: Calculated at checkout based on weight and destination
- Customs: Customer is responsible for any customs duties

## Shipping Rates
Shipping rates are calculated based on:
- Order weight
- Destination (domestic or international)
- Selected shipping method
- Current carrier rates

## Packaging
All orders are carefully packaged to prevent damage:
- Items are wrapped in protective materials
- Fragile items receive extra padding
- Packaging is recyclable and eco-friendly

## Delivery Address
- Shipments can be sent to any address in the continental United States
- PO Boxes are not accepted for Express or Overnight shipments
- Apartment numbers and suite numbers must be included

## Order Processing
- Orders are processed within 1 business day of placement
- Processing does not include weekends or holidays
- You will receive a shipping confirmation with tracking information

## Tracking Your Order
- Tracking information is provided via email
- You can also track your order in your account dashboard
- Most carriers offer SMS and email notifications

## Delivery Delays
We are not responsible for delays caused by:
- Weather conditions
- Natural disasters
- Carrier strikes or service disruptions
- Government actions

## Damaged or Lost Shipments
- Report damage or loss within 24 hours of delivery
- Provide photos and tracking number
- We will investigate and offer replacement or refund

## Return Shipping
See our Refund Policy for return shipping details. We provide prepaid return labels for eligible returns.

## Questions
Contact our shipping team at shipping@company.com for any questions about delivery.
